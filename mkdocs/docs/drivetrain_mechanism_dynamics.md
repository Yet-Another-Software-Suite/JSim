# Drivetrain & Mechanism Dynamics

This page summarizes models used for drivetrain and mechanism simulation.

Drivetrain
- Differential drive: wheel-ground interaction, slip, and torque limits
- Swerve/mecanum: kinematics and per-wheel friction models (when implemented)

Mechanisms
- Gear trains, belt-driven elements, and attached subsystems modeled with joint limits and actuator maps.

Tuneable parameters
- Motor models, gearbox ratios, friction, and damping values are exposed in model constructors.
